Sistem Penghubung Warga (SIPEWE)

(College Project)

The limited communication of residents in a village will be a problem and cause Miss Information or misunderstandings. With SIPIU residents can share information (in the form of posts) either visually or in writing, this application is equipped with an E-Voting feature that allows the RT/RW election process to run effectively and efficiently, and SIPIU is equipped with an event invitation feature where every citizen can make events and events known to all villagers.

Stack : Android studio Firebase




Sistem Pemantauan Infus & Urine Bag (SIPIU)

(Thesis Project)

Patients with kidney failure require intensive attention in performing intravenous treatment, because the balance between fluid intake and output in patients with kidney failure must be considered. SiPiu is a system that aims to monitor infusion fluids and urine bags of patients with kidney failure who are undergoing intensive infusion treatment at the hospital. Aims to facilitate nurses in maintaining the stability of fluid intake and output in patients.

Stack : Android studio Firebase NodeMCU ESP8266
